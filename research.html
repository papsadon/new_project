Software Developed

  1. DRM/DRM+ Transmitter

    2. Digital Radio Mondiale is a standard developed for digital audio broadcasting technologies. It's an openly standardized digital broadcasting system for all broadcasting frequencies, including LW, MW, SW, band I, II (FM band) and III(above 30 MHz). This technology is used to work over the AM broadcasting, especially shortwave. DRM is capable to fit more channels than AM of better quality & at low power utilization into a given amount of bandwidth. Development of DRM revolutionized the broadcasting technology & opens a new door to utilize the limited available bandwidth. Efficient use of bandwidth is possible due to availability of modern compression techniques & low cost processing through computers. That is the main principle behind the DRM.

  2. ई-ASHA

    3.   3. Link Budget Estimation for ship-to-ship communication

    4. The purpose of this research is to analyze, model and simulate the possible communication scenerios that occur on naval ships by exploring the possible solutions/ models. A traditional link budget for narrowband systems based on the Friis transmission formula, does not accurately predicts the received signal’s power in naval systems. The various models was incorporated for inputs such as frequency. Ship transmitters equipments, antenna Characteristic, Cables alternuation parameters, propagation models, and possible dynamics of platforms.
