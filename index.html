Hi, I'm Dr. Sandeep K. Yadav

Associate Professor at IIT Jodhpur

I am is Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur. I have received his PhD in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur in 2010. I am a recipient of SSI Young Scientist Award (2011), NI GSD Award (2012), IBM shared Research University Award (2014), and Teacher's Excellence Award (2020). My research interests include Signal Processing, Condition Monitoring, Communication systems, Image Processing, Blind Source Separation, Artificial Neural Network and Healthcare applications.
